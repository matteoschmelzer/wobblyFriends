## Question 2 -- Hypotheses
1. The use of gesture increases (in terms of numnber of gestures produced, complexity and gesture space used) when participants are unfamiliar with one another.

2. Participants that are familiar with one another have a higher success rate (more times successful and more quickly).

3. Participants on the balance board take longer due to additional resources allocated to balancing.


## Question 3 -- Dependent variable(s)
-   success rate, measured in distance of tokens until word is guessed correctly in taboo game
    -   additionally: duration (s)

-   (ideal world, larger study: manual labelling of individual attempts to compare to duration until success and tokens produced)
